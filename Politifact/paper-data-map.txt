PAPER EQUATION:
───────────────
t(u,v) = T_u × R_v

where:
  T_u = Number of real news items shared by user u / Total number of news items shared by user u
  R_v = ln(Total number of news / Number of times news item v is shared)

MEANING:
────────
• t(u,v) measures the trustworthiness-engagement score for user u and news item v
• Combines user reliability (T_u) with content popularity penalty (R_v)
• Higher scores → More trustworthy user + Less viral (unpopular) content
• Lower scores → Less trustworthy user + More viral (popular) content
• Paper states: "normalized on a 1 to 5 scale" (actual data: 0.5 to 4.0)
